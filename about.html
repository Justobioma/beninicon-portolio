Joseph Ajienka

Professor Ajienka, was the 7th Vice-Chancellor (2010-2015), University of Port Harcourt, Emmanuel Egbogah Chair and Professor of Petroleum Engineering, University of Port Harcourt. He was the pioneer Director of the Institute of Petroleum Studies (2003-2010). Head Department of Petroleum Engineering (1995-1997); member Governing Board of African Virtual University (AVU) Kenya (2013-2015). He was Chairman, Committee of Vice-Chancellors (CVC) of Federal Universities and Chairman Association of Vice-Chancellors of Nigerian Universities (AVCNU) 2014-2015. He is a member of the Board of the National Universities Commission (NUC) and the Board of National Oﬃce of Technology Acquisition and Promotion (NOTAP). He is a Director of PetroNet-Africa and member Board of Trustees of the Nigerian Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE). He is married with three children and grandchildren

Philomena Ofuafo

Dr. Philomina Ofuafo, a Senior lecturer in the Department of Religious Studies at the University of Lagos, is an accomplished academic. She holds a BA Hons from the University of Calabar, as well as an MA in African Studies and a PhD in (Iconography/Symbolism) in Religious studies both from the University of Ibadan. She is a member of various professional bodies, including the Academic Excellence Society of Nigeria, Society of Nigerian Artist, African Cultural Society of Arts, Religion in Society Research Network, International Society of Art, among others. Editor for several local and international journals. She is also a Fellow of Society of Research Academics (FRS). She has published over 30 articles in both local and international journals. Dr. Ofuafo has held various positions in the University and pious Christian societies and organisations,. Dr. Ofuafo is also a writer and has contributed to several research papers both locally and internationally. She has chaired numerous Conferences amongst is Pride Women Conference Dr. Mrs. Philomina Ofuafo is happily married with children.

Oni Akinsoji

Oni Akinsoji is a seasoned art Instructor, an art historian and (CEO) of Creative Branding and Printing Solutions. He started his art career at Oyo State College of Education now Emmanuel Alayande College of Education Oyo, before proceeding to Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun Sate where he obtained B.A. in Graphics and went to University of Ibadan for his Master degree in African Visual Art (Art history). He is currently lecturing Graphics and Art History at Lagos State Polytechnic, Ikorodu.

Oluwatoyin Vincent Adepoju

Adepoju is the founder and director of the online research and publication network Compcros: Comparative Cognitive Processes and Systems. My research focus is on the intersection of the visual and verbal arts, philosophy and spirituality in Africa, Asia, and the West. He lectured at the University of Benin from 1990-2002. He has a BA in English and Literature from the University of Benin and an MA from the School of Oriental and African Studies, University of London
